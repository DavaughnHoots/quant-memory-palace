How to Navigate

🖱️ Controls:

  • Left click + drag: Rotate view
  • Right click + drag: Pan camera
  • Scroll: Zoom in/out
  • Click document: View details

📁 Upload:

Drag and drop files or click the upload zone. Supports PDF, images, text, and audio files.


🔍 Search:

Type to search semantically. The camera will fly to relevant documents.


🌈 Visual Cues:

  • Colors: Different file types
  • Connections: Related documents
  • Clusters: Grouped by content similarity

📐 Shape Legend:

  • 📄 Cube: PDF documents
  • 🖼️ Plane: Images
  • 💎 Octahedron: Text files
  • 🎵 Cylinder: Audio files
  • 🎬 Cone: Video files
  • ⚪ Sphere: Other files
